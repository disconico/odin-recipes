Bakery Style Pizza

Description

Now found exclusively at one well-known pizza garden in Gravesend, Brooklyn, this style of pizza was once found in neighborhood bakeries. This pizza is light, crisp, and slightly spicy. Enjoy with a can of soda.

Ingredients

  1. Dough
    • warm water
    • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
    • 3 cups bread flour
    • 1 teaspoon fine salt
    • cup olive olive
  2. Sauce
    • 1 can plain crushed tomatoes
    • 1 can pizza sauce
  3. Cheese
    • 8 ounces low moisture whole-milk mozzarella, very thinly sliced
    • 1/4 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
